Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my router?

Unplug your router for 30 seconds, then plug it back in. Wait 3-5 minutes for it to fully restart.

Why is my internet slow?

Check for background downloads, restart your router, or contact support if the issue persists.

How do I pay my bill?

You can pay online through our website, M-Pesa, or visit our office. Bills are due on the 5th of each month.

Can I upgrade my package?

Yes, you can upgrade anytime. Contact our support team or use the customer portal to change your plan.
